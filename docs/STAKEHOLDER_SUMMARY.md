# Fieldtrip: Stakeholder Project Summary (May 2026)

## 1. Executive Overview
Fieldtrip is a mobile-first, seasonal expedition platform that gamifies urban observation. The "Summer 2026" season introduces the **Bureau Audit** loop, where users act as field scouts to document their immediate environment through a deck-based mission system.

## 2. Current Status: Pre-Beta (Stable)
The application has completed its core functional pass and is ready for the 5/29 Stakeholder Demo and 5/30 Beta Launch. All major components of the "Draw → Capture → Secure" cycle are operational.

## 3. What is Ready (Production-Grade)
- **The Deck**: A highly interactive, 3D-simulated card stack for mission discovery.
- **Mission Intelligence**: Standardized mission bank with 25+ diverse "field challenges" and "evidence challenges."
- **Capture Loop**: Functional viewfinder, multi-stage evidence validation (minimum 10-character field notes), and tiered scoring.
- **The Bureau Identity**: A consistent, high-contrast visual theme (Cosmic Slate) with custom typography and animations.
- **Scoring Engine**: XP calculation with logic for field-type bonuses, range bonuses, and "Bureau Penalties" (hint mitigation).

## 4. What is Simulated (Beta-Specific)
- **Location Sync**: For the current beta stability, the "Satellite Lock" is automated (simulated verification) to ensure high completion rates during initial device testing.
- **Image Processing**: Custom "Lens Filters" are emulated in-browser. Production-grade server-side RAW rendering is a post-beta priority.
- **Asset Fidelity**: Some sticker/badge icons use "Summer Alpha" placeholders until final holographic assets are released in June.

## 5. Stakeholder Milestones
- **5/29 Presentation**: A Live Field Demo showing a mission completion from draw to finish. **Goal**: Demonstrate the unique aesthetic and clear user loop.
- **5/30 Beta Launch**: Invite-only release to 50 scouts. **Goal**: Validate usability across diverse mobile devices and browser environments.

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Mission Completion Rate**: Percentage of users who finish the draw-to-secure loop successfully.
- **Engagement Depth**: Total number of missions archived per user during the 7-day test window.
- **Fun Factor**: qualitative benchmark from the Beta Feedback Checklist.

## 7. Next Priorities (June 2026)
- **Production GPS**: Enabling deep geofencing and real-world distance verification.
- **The Crew Hub**: Expanding the multi-player "Crew Territory" and competitive leaderboards.
- **Holographic Vault**: Releasing high-fidelity animated rewards and archival artifacts.
