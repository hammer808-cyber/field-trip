# Beta Feedback Checklist

Please answer the following questions after completing your field test. Use "Yes/No" and provide brief comments on anything that felt confusing or "off."

## 1. Deck Page (The Hub)
- **First Impression**: Was it immediately obvious what you needed to do first?
- **Visual Feel**: Did the animations (grid effects, scanlines, floating cards) enhance the experience or distract you?
- **Clarity**: Did you understand your "status" (tokens remaining, rank progress) correctly?
- **Archive**: Was it easy to find and review your past missions?

## 2. Mission Selection
- **The "Ask"**: Were the mission objectives written in a way that made sense?
- **Checklist**: Was the evidence requirement checklist clear before you started?
- **Interest**: Did the missions feel fun or interesting to attempt?
- **Hints**: Did the "Bureau Penalty" for using a hint seem fair and understandable?

## 3. Capture Flow
- **Viewfinder**: Did the camera interface feel responsive?
- **Development**: Did the "Developing..." animation add to the mood, or was it too long?
- **Note Input**: Was the field note prompt visible and helpful while you were typing?
- **Validation**: If you missed a requirement, did the "INCOMPLETE PROOF" message help you fix it?

## 4. Secure Evidence & Results
- **Transmission**: Did the submission feel reliable?
- **XP Clarity**: Was it clear how your final score was calculated?
- **Math Wizard**: (If active) Did the detailed point breakdown add value or confusion?
- **Satisfaction**: Did the result screen feel like a "win"?

## 5. Mobile & UI
- **Clipped Elements**: Did any buttons or text overlap or get cut off on your screen? (Please specify your device).
- **Navigation**: Was it always easy to find your way back "home"?
- **Horizontal Scrolling**: Did you experience any accidental horizontal swiping that felt broken?

## 6. Closing Thoughts
- **Fun Factor**: Would you want to keep playing this app for multiple days?
- **Top 1 Fix**: If you could change exactly one thing about the flow, what would it be?
