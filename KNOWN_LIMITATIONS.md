# FIELDTRIP BETA: KNOWN LIMITATIONS

This document outlines the current boundaries and known technical limits of the Fieldtrip Beta.

## 1. Feature Scope
- **Active Decks**: This beta is limited to **Starter Signals**, **Heatwave Receipts**, and **SoCal Summer**. Other deck prototypes are currently inactive.
- **Admin Review**: Review is manual for this phase. Approval may take between 10 minutes to 2 hours during active testing windows.

## 2. Technical Dependencies
- **AI Detection**: The AI "Evidence Detector" provides real-time feedback during capture. If it fails to identify your subject, continue with submission—admins will perform manual verification.
- **Permissions**: **Camera** and **Location** are required for proof capture. If you deny these, you will be unable to complete missions.
- **Image Formats**: Standard JPG/PNG files are supported. Some specific formats (like high-res RAW) may fail to upload depending on network conditions.

## 3. Data & Accounts
- **Single Account**: Please do not create multiple accounts with the same email.
- **Persistence**: While we aim to keep your progress, database "Resets" may occur during major beta updates.
- **XP & Rewards**: Points and rankings are indicative of the beta environment and do not carry over to full launch.

## 4. Interaction Hazards
- **Double Submission**: Do not attempt to "Spam" the submit button if the network is slow. One tap is sufficient; the app will sync when ready.
- **Pending Lock**: You cannot restart or edit a mission that is currently in the **Pending Review** queue.

## 5. Visuals & Copy
- **UI Slop**: You may see placeholder icons or debug labels in some admin menus.
- **Tone**: Some mission descriptions are still being adjusted for clarity.

*Thank you for helping us find the edges of the simulation.*
