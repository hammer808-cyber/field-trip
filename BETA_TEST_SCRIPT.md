# FIELDTRIP BETA TESTER SCRIPT

Welcome to the Fieldtrip Beta! This script will guide you through the core experience. Please follow these steps and note any friction or confusion.

## Phase A: Initial Deployment
1. **Open the App**: Launch the provided URL in your mobile browser (Safari on iOS or Chrome on Android recommended).
2. **Onboarding**: 
   - Accept the age and legal confirmation.
   - You will see requests for **Camera** and **Location**. These are used to capture mission proof and verify context. Please allow these to test the full flow.
3. **Field Type Quiz**: Answer the questions naturally. Confirm your result (e.g., "The Urbanist", "The Sentinel") and tap **Start App**.

## Phase B: Starter Signals
1. **Navigate to Missions**: Tap the 'Deck' or 'Missions' icon in the navigation bar.
2. **Draw a Mission**: Confirm that "Starter Signals" is the first available deck. Tap to draw your first mission.
3. **Begin Mission**: Review the mission bio and prompt. Tap **Start Mission**.
4. **Capture Proof**:
   - Tap the camera icon to take a photo or upload one from your library.
   - Add a brief **Field Note** (e.g., "Found near the park entrance").
5. **Submit**: Tap **Submit Proof**. Confirm that the mission status updates to **Pending Review**.

## Phase C: Admin Review Simulation
For the purpose of this beta, an admin will periodically review submissions.
1. **Wait for Review**: Do not attempt to resubmit or delete the mission while it is pending.
2. **Scenario: Needs More Proof**: If your mission is returned for "More Proof", test the **Fix Proof** flow.
3. **Scenario: Rejected**: If rejected, test the **Retry Mission** feature.
4. **Scenario: Approved**: Once approved, note your XP gain and progress.

## Phase D: Decks & Unlocks
1. **Unlock Requirement**: Note that the "Heatwave Receipts" and "SoCal Summer" decks are locked initially.
2. **Verification**: Confirm they unlock **only** after you have 3 approved missions in the Starter Signals deck.
3. **Explore**: Once unlocked, draw missions from these new decks and confirm the "SoCal Summer" theme and missions appear correctly.

## Phase E: What to Report
Please keep a log of:
- **Confusion**: "I didn't know what to tap next."
- **Clarity**: "The permission prompt for location was unclear."
- **Bugs**: "The app crashed when I uploaded a HEIC image."
- **Layout**: "The button is cut off on my iPhone SE screen."
- **Loop**: "I got stuck in the quiz results page."

**Send all feedback and screenshots to your beta coordinator!**
